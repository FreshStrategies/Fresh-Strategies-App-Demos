How the forecast works

Northbay forecasts packaging by velocity, not by a bill of materials. Each item's weekly burn is the trailing pull rate from the ERP, smoothed over 13 weeks and adjusted by the day-of-week profile and any seasonal uplift. Because packaging maps cleanly to case volume, this beats trying to explode a BOM per SKU — and it recomputes the moment on-hand or inbound changes.
